How to be healthy in 2020/ by Atopos

当范文使吧，但不要全抄，注意控制度

At the beginning of the year 2020, COVID-19 broke out and caused much trouble in our daily life. Many people felt scared. In order to stay away from it, You need to do the following things.
To protect ourselves, first of all, we should wash our hands more, open the window more and keep the air clear.
Next, it's a good idea to take our temperature every day, because it can help us to check our health faster. At the same time, doing more exercise and keeping great eating habits are good for our health too.
Then, to avoid infecting the virus, taking the bus and going to where there are many people less are very important because you can't be sure whether there are people infected with the virus in the crowd.
Besides, if you have to go to the place with many people, you must wear a mask and avoid talking with somebody face to face.
Last but not least, If you have to got a fever or cold, don t worry about it, going to the hospital to see a doctor will make you and your family safer and healthier.
I hope you can stay away from CDVID-19, please protect each other!
